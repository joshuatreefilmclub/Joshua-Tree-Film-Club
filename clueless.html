Review No. 3: Clueless (1995) - Teen/Comedy

A Teen Classic - Watched on 8/26/2025

Directed by:Amy Heckerling
Starring: Alicia Silverstone, Stacey Dash

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★★★☆☆ (3 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

This film was a crazy experience. Normally, I am not really into these type of cliche teenager films but I had fun watching this. Firstly, all of the actors playing in this movie are very talented and the story was very engaging. I loved the script and it had lots of funny dialogues and skits that worked very well.

The characters can at sometimes feel a bit frustrating and the story isn't very realistic and at certain points doesn't make sense. But overall I had a good time watching it. It's not a film where you have to sit down and pay close attention. The goal of this movie is just to relax and not overthink anything. The reason why I didn't give it higher than 3 stars was just that this isn't normally the time of film that I would watch and find great. The production of the movie itself was decent and the scenery wasn't anything crazy.
