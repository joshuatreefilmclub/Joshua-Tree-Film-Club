Review No. 6: Dead Poets Society (1989) - Drama/Emotional

I'm bout to cry- Watched on 9/13/2025

Directed by: Peter Weir
Starring:Robin Williams, Robert Sean Leonard, Ethan Hawke

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

Wow this movie was so emotional, I was not ready for this. Dead Poets Society is a movie, set in a competitive private schools in the middle of nowhere in 1980s America. Gosh, I related so much to this movie, as someone who also goes to a competitive high-school. The movie deals a lot with parental relationships, existentialism, and the search for meaning in ones life. The actors are so good in thi movie especially Robin Williams and Ethan Hawke did such an amazing performance. This film really taught me a lot and I appreciate it a lot.
