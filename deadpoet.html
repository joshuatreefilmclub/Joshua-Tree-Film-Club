Review No. 6: Dead Poets Society (1989) - Drama/Emotional

I'm bout to cry- Watched on 9/13/2025

Directed by: Peter Weir
Starring:Robin Williams, Robert Sean Leonard, Ethan Hawke

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

Today I watched a movie called Dead Poets Society. I was not prepared for this flick, as it was very emotionally captivating and had strong themes that demanded my full attention. This movie isn't easy to watch but it is very powerful.

The actors were very good especially Ethan Hawke and Robert Williams who clearly captivated the emotions of studying in a competitive boarding school in the countryside of America in the 50s. I realted a lot to this movie as I also go to a high school where the levels of competition can feel very toxicating and existential thinking comes easy to the mind. I think the pacing of this movie, makes it so that the movie doesn't feel dull or anything like that. I also enjoyed the story elements and actually just the entire story. For lots of movie its easy to find holes in the plot or question story-telling choices, but for Dead Poets Society its hard to find anything wrong in the script.

Overall I would strongly recommend this movie to anybody. I believe this is one of the films that everybody should see in their lifetime.
