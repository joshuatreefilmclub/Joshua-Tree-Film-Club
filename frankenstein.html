Review No. 10: Frankenstein (1931) - Horror/Classic

BOO! - Watched on 10/31/2025

Directed by:James Whale
Starring: Colin Clive, Mae Clarke

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

After having watched Dracula in my previous review, my hopes were kind of down for continuing the Universal Monsters franchise. But I somehow decided for Halloween (mainly because they are relatively short), I first started the night with Frankenstein. Frankenstein was made the same year as Dracula, it has that same dull pace, but it wasn't as bad. I thought that Frankenstein looked really well for the time and was genuinely believable. The story itself was alright and not that memorable (im sure that people who read the book would say that the book was better). But the last scene in the movie, was absolutely cool the fire and the traditional village. What a memorable scene. My night started well and I couldn't wait to see what I would watch next.
