Review No. 2: The Fantastic 4: First Steps (2025) - Superhero/Comedy

Superhero films are back - Watched on 8/17/2025

Directed by: Matt Shakman
Starring: Pedro Pascal, Vanessa Kirby

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

Superhero films are so back this summer, with the newly released Superman (which I have yet to see) and the new Fantastic Four film. I don't know where to start for the Fantastic Four film, because it was amazing.

I loved all of the costume designs and the overall aesthetic of the movie set in the 1960s. I think that the CGI is very good. At first when I saw the images of Thing, I had some mixed feelings about it, but overall it's really great and Galactus was really well done.

But what really makes Groundhog Day a great film and not just a flat comedy movie, is that it blends philosophy and humor so effortlessly. It's rare to find a movie that makes you laugh, then genuinely reflect on your own life, makes you think, and laugh again

As someone who honestly isn't crazy about superhero movies because I find the pacing dull and the plot quite simplistic and unrealistic. This movie did a great job in managing the time and the dialogue was great. But especially the chemistry between the actors was so insynched. This is by far the best portrayal of the Fantastic Four in film.
