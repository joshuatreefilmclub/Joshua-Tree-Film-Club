Review No. 7: The Secret Life of Walter Mitty (2013) - Comedy/Adventure

Iceland seems fun - Watched on 9/23/2025

Directed by:Ben Stiller
Starring: Ben Stiller, Adam Scott, Kristen Wiig

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★★★★⯪ (4.5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

Today I watched a movie called The Secret Life of Walter Mitty and it was a really fun watch. The Secret Life of Walter Mitty is a very funny comedy movie with SNL alumni Ben Stiller. This movie was great for multiple reasons. The cinematography and the soundtrack carried the movie hard, with its fast comedic pacing, and a recognizable soundtrack. Ben Stiller is an amazing actor and it was really fun watching him. I know that he is involved with lots of crappy comedic movies, but this film is really great!

I liked the story about an employee that works at TIME magazines during the period that they are starting to switch to being an online platform. The setting being in New York and Iceland is really fun. It makes me want to go to Iceland now.

Overall I recommend this film and I hope that people aren't scared just because the main actor is Ben Stiller (he also directed the movie!). Because he really put his heart into this movie as Walter Mitty and its worth a good watch.
