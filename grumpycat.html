Review No. 3: Grumpy Cat’s Worst Christmas Ever (2014) - Family / Comedy

A Meme Film - Watched on 8/6/2025

Directed by: Tim Hill
Starring: Aubrey Plaza (voice of Grumpy Cat)

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★½☆☆☆ (1.5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

Although this movie isn’t as bad as I thought it was, and isn’t the worst internet film ever, it is still a bad movie. Grumpy Cat’s Worst Christmas Ever takes a famous meme from the mid-2010s and stretches it into an 80-minute film.

I respect that the movie knows it’s bad and doesn’t pretend otherwise. It breaks the fourth wall and mocks itself, which adds a tiny bit of charm. I have more empathy for movies that know they’re a joke than for ones that take themselves seriously.

The acting isn’t perfect, but Aubrey Plaza’s voice acting grew on me. The humor is dated, relying heavily on 2014 internet jokes, most of which don’t land today.

The script is messy, and the story doesn’t hold together. It’s a strange mashup of holiday clichés, mall chases, and talking animals, all with awkward pacing. The “heartwarming” moments feel forced.

Final Thought: Honestly, I just hope nobody decides to revive this film and leave it alone without a sequel.
