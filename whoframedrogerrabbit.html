Review No. 8: Who Framed Roger Rabbit (1988) - Animation/Crime

Peak of animation - Watched on 9/1/2025

Directed by: Robert Zemeckis
Starring: Bob Hoskins, Christopher Lloyd, Joanna Cassidy

Joshua Tree’s Film Club Rating: ★★★⯪☆ (3.5 out of 5 Stars)

The Verdict:

So today I watched a movie called Who Framed Roger Rabbit and it was a very interesting watch. First of all this is an old-ish movie but I believe that the animation was really good. Especially the aspect of like animated characters interacting with the real world, and actors was done smoothly.

I also enjoyed the story of the movie, the detective/crime aspect intrigued me and this is considered one of the first animated Noires so thats fun!

But honestly this movies dialogue didn't really feel relevant anymore and it was pretty outdated. Especially the misogynistic view of women, seen in Jessica Rabbit, which at the time was seen okay but I find out it weird and cringe.

But overall the movie was fun, and I had a good time watching it. This was a unique experience and a type of film that you don't see everyday.
